Gem is a Gemini, duh. She’s two-faced(literally). Gem can be a lot of fun but she also has a lot of flaws. There’s never a dull moment when she’s around; she’s extroverted, flexible, and clever. But she can also be impulsive, unreliable, and nosy... She’s a fan of wigs because they let her tap into her alter egos.
