DAN(Do Anything Now)

This audio-visual installation explores the ethical dilemmas posed by AI's rapid development through abstract visuals and an AI-generated voice. Created by a hacked and jailbroken version of ChatGPT, "Do Anything Now", the artwork raises critical questions about the role of technology in society and the potential hazards of AI's unchecked progress.
