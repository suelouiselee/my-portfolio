sometimes i move so fast i become what's around me(2023)

+ interactive software

+ P5.js, JavaScript, HTML, Sonic Pi, Audacity

Hey, Can you pick me up? I'm scared. I'm at the Intersection of Art and Technology: UCLA DMA Undergrad 2023 (Exhibition). A state of limbo emerges when the speed of our environment is out of sync with our perception of it. In it exists flux and perpetual transformation. We can easily become lost and vanish in the endless flow of information we call experience. The audio element is a compilation of AI-generated statements of empathy and rhythmic markers. The interactive element uses motion detection to alter and challenge the coherence of one's physical image, bringing attention to the role motion plays in our understanding of time and harmony with the external world.
