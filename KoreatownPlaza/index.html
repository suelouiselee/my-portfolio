Koreatown Plaza(2024)

+ 3D modeling, texturing, rendering

+ Maya

Koreatown Plaza was established in 1988 as the first major indoor shopping mall in Koreatown, Los Angeles. My family shopped there every Sunday, and if I was lucky, I’d visit Artbox to add to my food eraser collection. Frozen in the '80s, the stores are mostly empty and receive little foot traffic. This 3D model depicts my favorite corner of the mall.
