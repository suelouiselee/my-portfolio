Koreatown Plaza(2024)

+ 3D modeling, texturing, rendering

+ Maya

Koreatown Plaza was established in 1988 as the first major indoor shopping center in Koreatown, Los Angeles. Growing up my family would go grocery shopping there every Sunday and if I got lucky, I could go to Artbox and grow my food eraser collection. The mall is straight out of the 80s and holds no semblance to malls in South Korea, and today, the stores are mostly empty and receive little foot traffic. This is a 3D model of Koreatown Plaza from memory.
